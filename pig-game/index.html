Instructions 🐖

👥 This is a two player game, played in rounds.

🎲 On player's turn, they roll a dice as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their round score.

🎲 The player can choose to hold, which adds their round score to their global score and ends their turn.

❗ If the player rolls a 1, their round score is lost, and they lose their turn

❗ If the player rolls two 6's in a row, their global score is lost, and they lose their turn

🥇 The first player whose global score reaches the winning score, wins the game. The winning score is set to 100 by default.
